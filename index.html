About

oblivious film is an app for creating films from 1-second moments cut from your photos and videos.

Even ordinary moments can begin to reveal a flow, meaning, or emotion you did not notice at the time, simply through the way they are arranged and layered.

Overview

Use it for daily memories, travel moments, or promotional storytelling. There is no single way to use it.

Your fragments can become your own story.
